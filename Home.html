Our Philosophy

Crafting Memorable Dining Journeys

Founded in 2018, The Velvet Bistro has quickly established itself as a cornerstone of fine dining. We believe in honoring the natural flavors of high-quality local produce while pushing boundaries with contemporary techniques.

Whether you're celebrating a monumental milestone or dropping in for a casual evening glass of wine, our team is dedicated to treating you like family.
